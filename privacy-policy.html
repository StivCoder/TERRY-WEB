1. Introduction

Welcome to Terry's Tender Wear ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand our practices regarding any personal information we collect from you.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we use that information
  • How we protect your data
  • Your rights and choices

By accessing or using our website, you accept the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

WhatsApp Orders: When you order via WhatsApp, we collect your name, phone number, message content, product selections, and preferences you share with us.

Payment Information: For M-Pesa, bank transfers, or cash orders, we collect transaction details, payment method, and payment confirmation numbers. We do not process or store credit card information directly.

Contact Forms: When you contact us through our website contact form, we collect your name, email, phone number, subject, and message.

Customer Accounts: If you create an account, we collect your name, email, phone number, delivery address, and order history.

Reviews & Feedback: If you submit a review or feedback, we collect your name, location, review content, and rating.

B. Information Collected Automatically

Website Analytics: We use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source
  • Approximate location (country/city level)

Cookies: We use cookies to remember your preferences, shopping cart, and login information. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our site (device type, screen size, etc.).

C. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Payment processors (M-Pesa, banks)
  • Delivery partners (for shipping information)
  • WhatsApp (message metadata only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To confirm, ship, and deliver your orders
  • Payment Processing: To process payments and prevent fraud
  • Customer Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Order Updates: To send delivery tracking, shipping updates, and order confirmations
  • Improvements: To analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Marketing: To send promotional offers, new arrivals, and newsletters (only with your consent)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Analytics: To understand customer behavior and optimize our website

4. WhatsApp & Messaging Privacy

Important: WhatsApp is a third-party service owned by Meta. When you message us on WhatsApp:

  • Your messages are encrypted end-to-end by WhatsApp
  • WhatsApp may collect metadata (who messaged whom, when)
  • We cannot guarantee privacy beyond what WhatsApp provides
  • Please review WhatsApp's privacy policy separately

Our Responsibility: We will:

  • Only use WhatsApp for order processing and customer support
  • Not share your WhatsApp messages with third parties without permission
  • Delete message history after order completion (or upon request)
  • Keep your phone number confidential

Opting Out: You can message "STOP" on WhatsApp to be removed from our WhatsApp mailing list.

5. Data Storage & Security

Where We Store Data: Your data is stored on secure servers. We currently use Supabase for database management, which uses encrypted cloud storage.

Security Measures: We protect your information through:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure password hashing for accounts
  • Limited access to customer data (authorized staff only)
  • Regular security monitoring
  • Compliance with data protection standards

Data Retention: We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request data deletion at any time.

No Guarantee: While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against all security breaches.

6. When We Share Information

We will NOT sell your personal data. We only share information when necessary:

  • Delivery Partners: Your name, phone, and address for shipping
  • Payment Processors: Transaction information for payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or government authority
  • Service Providers: Analytics tools, email services (under confidentiality agreements)

We do not share your information for marketing purposes without explicit consent.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (with limitations for legal/accounting purposes)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications anytime
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

How to Exercise Rights: Contact us at hello@terrys-tenderwear.co.ke with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, questions, or requests, please contact us:

  • WhatsApp: +254 118 053 640
  • Email: hello@terrys-tenderwear.co.ke
  • Address: Nairobi, Kenya

We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 7 business days.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📌 Note: This privacy policy is designed for Terry's Tender Wear's specific operations. Laws vary by jurisdiction. If you are outside Kenya and have concerns, please consult local privacy regulations.
