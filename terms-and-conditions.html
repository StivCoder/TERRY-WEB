7. Returns & Refunds

Our Return Policy: We offer hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery.

Return Conditions

Items must be:

  • Unused and unwashed
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Not damaged or stained

Return Process

To return an item:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We will arrange a pickup or provide return instructions
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund

Refund Timeline

  • M-Pesa: 1-2 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 2-5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Cash refund at pickup

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items washed or worn
  • Items with damage beyond normal handling
  • Items without original tags
  • Items purchased at a final sale or clearance price

Exchange vs. Refund

Size Issues: If you ordered the wrong size, we offer free size exchanges within 7 days. Simply message us your order number and the correct size. We will arrange a swap at no extra cost.

Defective Items: If you receive a defective or damaged item, we will replace it immediately at no cost. Please provide photos of the damage.

💡 Tip: Always refer to our Size Guide before ordering to ensure the correct fit. Sizes are true to our guide and designed with extra room for nappies.
